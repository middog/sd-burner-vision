## The Golden Rules

1. **Show up.** That's 90% of it.
2. **Participate.** Don't be a spectator.
3. **Gift freely.** Give without expecting return.
4. **Ask questions.** Nobody knows everything.
5. **Help others.** We all lift together.
6. **Clean up.** Leave no trace.
7. **Don't be a gatekeeper.** We were all new once.

---

## If You Feel Lost

**Normal.** Everyone feels lost at first. This community has history, inside jokes, and established patterns. It takes time.

**Things that help:**
- Keep showing up (faces become familiar)
- Ask people their story (everyone loves to share)
- Find one person you click with (they'll introduce you to others)
- Volunteer for something small (you'll meet people who do things)

**Things that don't help:**
- Trying to know everything before participating
- Waiting to be invited (just show up)
- Comparing yourself to people who've been here years
