## How They Work Together

These frameworks aren't separate â€” they layer:

| Framework | Question |
|-----------|----------|
| **Fire Triangle** | What's *missing*? (Heat, Oxygen, or Fuel) |
| **Growth Path** | What *kind* of thing is this? (Project, Party, or Practice) |
| **3S Stage** | How *mature* is the work? (Sense, Stabilize, or Strengthen) |

**Example:**

> "We want to start a regular sewing night."

- **Fire Triangle:** Do we have heat (people interested)? Oxygen (someone to coordinate)? Fuel (machines and space)? â†’ Looks like we need more **oxygen** â€” nobody's stepped up to organize.
- **Growth Path:** This is a **party** right now. If it keeps happening, it could become a **practice**.
- **3S Stage:** We're still **sensing** â€” we don't know how many people want this or what night works.

**Next step:** Post in Discord to gauge interest (sense), then pick a date and owner (stabilize), then actually host one (strengthen).

## Fire Party: Where 3s Come Alive ğŸ”¥ğŸ‰

A **Fire Party** is a 45-60 minute working session where we use all three frameworks to move work forward.

It's not a status meeting. It's a **momentum session**.

The agenda is â€” you guessed it â€” three parts:

### 1. Sense (10 min)
- Scan new issues
- Ask: What's the real problem? Who's affected?
- Quick-tag with labels

*Don't solve yet. Just see.*

### 2. Stabilize (20 min)
- Confirm what stage things are at
- Define "done" for priority items
- Assign owners (or flag as help-wanted)
- Set next touch dates

*Lock things down.*

### 3. Strengthen (15 min)
- Pick 1-3 items to advance *right now*
- Actually do the work together
- Pair up if helpful

*Not "plan to do later." Do it now.*
