## Current Projects

CoLab is home to dozens of ongoing projects—art cars, sculptures, interactive installations, and more.

## Start Your Project

1. **Come to Open Hours** — Tour the space, meet people
2. **Get Oriented** — Complete safety orientation
3. **Claim Space** — Talk to a coordinator about storage
4. **Build** — Use the tools, take classes, ask for help
