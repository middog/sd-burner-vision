## Step 1: Visit

Come to Open Hours:
- **First Fridays:** 6–9 PM (monthly open house)
- **Sunday Open Shop:** 12–5 PM (weekly)

Tour the space. Meet people. See what's possible.

## Step 2: Orient

Complete the safety orientation (about 1 hour). You'll learn:
- Space rules and expectations
- Safety equipment locations
- Tool access levels
- How to get help

After orientation, you can use basic tools during staffed hours.

## Step 3: Learn

Take classes to unlock access to specific tools. Each class teaches safe operation and certifies you for unsupervised use.

## Step 4: Build

Use the space during open hours. Take more classes. Ask for help. Make your thing.

## Costs

- **Open hours:** Free
- **Classes:** $20–50
- **Monthly contribution:** $50 suggested
- **Key holder (24/7):** $100/month

Can't afford it? We'd rather have you building than not. Talk to us.
