## What is CoLab?

CoLab is a community makerspace for Burning Man art and beyond. We provide the tools, space, and expertise to help you build your projects.

## Location

Barrio Logan area of San Diego. Address provided after orientation.

## Hours

- **First Fridays:** 6–9 PM (monthly open house)
- **Sunday Open Shop:** 12–5 PM (weekly)
- **Staffed Hours:** Tue/Thu 6–10 PM, Sat 10 AM–6 PM

## Costs

- **Open hours:** Free (donations welcome)
- **Classes:** $20–50 (covers materials)
- **Monthly contribution:** $50/month suggested
- **Key holder:** $100/month (24/7 access)

Can't afford it? Talk to us.
