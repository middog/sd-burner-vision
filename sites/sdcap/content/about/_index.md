## Overview

San Diego Collaborative Arts Project (SDCAP) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit supporting the San Diego Burning Man community through grants, governance, and coordination.

## What We Provide

- **Grants** — Funding for community art projects
- **Fiscal Sponsorship** — Tax-deductible donations for projects
- **Insurance** — Coverage for community events
- **YOUtopia** — Production of San Diego's regional burn
