## Overview

SDCAP provides grants to support community art aligned with Burning Man principles.

## Grant Programs

### YOUtopia Art Grants
- **Timeline:** Applications open March, decisions in May
- **Range:** $500 – $5,000
- **Requirements:** Project installed at YOUtopia, interactive, artist present

### Community Project Grants
- **Timeline:** Rolling applications, reviewed quarterly
- **Range:** $250 – $2,000
- **Eligible:** Pride floats, Maker Faire, public art, community workshops

## How to Apply

1. Prepare proposal (description, budget, timeline, team)
2. Submit via online form
3. Grants Committee reviews
4. Decision within 4 weeks
5. 70% disbursed on approval, 30% on completion

## What We Don't Fund

- Private/commercial projects
- Personal travel or tickets
- Already completed work
- Projects without community benefit
