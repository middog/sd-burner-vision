## Structure

SDCAP is governed by an all-volunteer Board of Directors (5-9 members, 2-year terms). Board positions include President, Vice President, Treasurer, Secretary, and Directors-at-Large.

## Transparency

As a 501(c)(3), we're committed to transparency:

- Financial reports available on request
- Board meetings open to observers
- Grant decisions include public rationale
- Annual report summarizes activities

## Get Involved

- **Attend meetings** — Monthly, open to community
- **Join a committee** — Grants, YOUtopia, Communications
- **Run for the board** — Elections at Annual Meeting
