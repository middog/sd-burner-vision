## Ways to Help

### Apply for a Grant
Have a project idea? [Apply for funding](/grants/).

### Join a Committee
- **Grants Committee** — Review applications
- **YOUtopia Committee** — Plan the regional burn
- **Communications** — Outreach and documentation

### Run for the Board
Elections at the Annual Meeting. Email board@sdcap.org to express interest.

### Donate
Support community art. Donations may be tax-deductible.

### Volunteer
YOUtopia and other events always need help.
